Otters Hold Hands While They Sleep

Fun fact number one about otters. They hold hands while they sleep so that if they float down river they at least stay together and have each other. One reason why otters are so cute.

Otters Mate for Life

Another reason why otters are so amazing is that they like humans mate for life. Not only do they mate for life, but their families are vital to their survival and so important to them, just like us.
